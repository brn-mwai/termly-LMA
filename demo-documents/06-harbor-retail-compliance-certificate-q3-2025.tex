\textbf{\textcolor{breach}{BREACH EXPLANATION:}}\\[0.2cm]
The Interest Coverage Ratio of 1.80x is below the required minimum of 2.00x. This breach is attributable to:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Declining same-store sales (-8.2\% YoY) due to increased e-commerce competition
    \item Rising interest rates increasing debt service costs by \$680,000 annually
    \item Store closure costs in Q2 2025 (\$1.2M one-time charge)
    \item Inventory write-downs of \$850,000 related to seasonal merchandise
\end{itemize}
\vspace{0.2cm}
\textbf{Remediation Plan:} Management has engaged restructuring advisors and is in active discussions with the lender regarding a potential covenant waiver and amendment.
